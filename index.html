What is a RickRoll?... A RickRoll is a modern day practical joke. "Rick Rolling" occurs when one person sends a link, video, or audio of the song "Never Gonna Give You Up" by Rick Astley, while making them think they are going to see or hear something completely different! This practical joke began on a social media site called 4chan in the videogames section. While the offical trailer for "Grand Theft Auto:San Andreas" had just came out, the site was flooded with users and ended up crashing, meaning users were unable to view the trailer. This is when an anonymous 4channer posted a link saying it was the trailer, when it ended up being the very first RickRoll. Since that time, the RickRoll continues to be one of the most well known pranks and can be seen on almost any social media platform such as TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, Tumblr, or Facebook.
